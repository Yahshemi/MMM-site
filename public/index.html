    Our Story.


    Motivated Military Movers was founded in 2008 by a Navy Corpsman. MMM's mission from the beginning of its start was always to give a excellent Move Service by saving customers the most money and time on their move while protecting their belongings. 13 years later, MMM is just as passionate about accomplishing our mission and we will always do that, we hold the belief of quality over quantity.


    Why Us.


    Motivated Military Movers is more than just a Moving company, We are a Veteran owned organization with a goal to hire a 100% Military & Veterans. Transitioning from the Military can be a difficult process; Being around other Veterans, getting immediate employment while learning a new trade and using your military skills & traits makes it much easier getting out. Its also super rewarding for our customers that hire MMM as moving is one of the most stressful things you can do in life, When you have Movers that are professional, trustworthy, hardworking and honest moving can be a great experience
